About Our Financial Calculators

Snowball Debt Calculator

The Snowball Calculator helps you repay debts strategically using 3 repayment methods:

  • Snowball Method: Pay off the smallest debt first while making minimum payments on the rest.
  • Avalanche Method: Focus on the debt with the highest interest rate first to reduce long-term costs.
  • Fewest Payments: Target the debt with the fewest remaining payments first to free up your budget quickly.

Combine this with our budgeting calculator to make the most efficient plan for debt freedom.
